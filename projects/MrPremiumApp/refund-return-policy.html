6. Return Policy for Digital Products

6.1 Digital Product Returns

Since digital products cannot be physically returned, "returns" are handled as refunds. Once a license or activation code is used, it cannot be returned. However, unused codes within 7 days can be refunded.

6.2 Code/License Management

If you wish to return an unused product:

  • Activation code must not have been used or redeemed
  • Request must be within 7 days of purchase
  • Provide proof of non-use
  • No technical support for unredeemed codes is required

6.3 Replacement vs Refund

For defective or incorrect products, we offer:

  • Replacement: New, functional code or product (at no cost) - preferred option
  • Refund: Full refund if replacement is not available - only if requested

We recommend choosing replacement first, as it's processed faster.

7. Special Circumstances

7.1 Subscription Products

For subscription-based products:

  • One-time subscriptions: Refund available if unused within 7 days
  • Recurring subscriptions: Cancel anytime; future charges will not be made
  • Already charged cycles: Only refundable if unused
  • Cancellation must be within 7 days of purchase to avoid next billing cycle

7.2 Bundle Purchases

For product bundles or combo packages:

  • If one item in bundle is defective: Replacement code for that item or proportional refund
  • If bundle pricing applies: Refund based on full bundle unless partial return is available
  • Bundle codes cannot be separated - refund is all-or-nothing

7.3 Promotional/Discounted Purchases

Products purchased with promotional codes, discounts, or special offers:

  • Refunded at the discounted price paid (not original price)
  • Promotional codes cannot be reused after refund
  • Discount codes expire upon refund processing

7.4 International Customers

Refunds for purchases from outside Bangladesh:

  • Refund policy remains the same
  • Exchange rate fluctuations are at customer's risk
  • Refunds issued in original currency paid
  • International transfer times may be longer
