5. Refund Process for Cancelled Orders

5.1 Automatic Refunds

For certain cancellations, refunds are automatic:

  • Cancelled before payment: No refund needed
  • Payment failed during checkout: No charge
  • We initiate return automatically upon cancellation approval

5.2 Manual Refund Process

For post-delivery cancellations:

  1. You submit cancellation request with order details
  2. We verify that product hasn't been used
  3. We confirm cancellation eligibility (within 7 days)
  4. We process refund to original payment method
  5. You receive refund confirmation via email

5.3 Refund Amount

Refund amount depends on cancellation timing:

  • Before Delivery: 100% of paid amount
  • After Delivery (Unused): 100% of product price
  • With Discount Applied: Refunded amount is based on discounted price (not original price)
  • Promotional Bundles: Entire bundle refunded or no refund if used

5.4 Refund Method

Refunds are processed to the original payment method:

  • bKash payments ‚Üí refunded to bKash account
  • Nagad payments ‚Üí refunded to Nagad account
  • Rocket payments ‚Üí refunded to Rocket account

Processing time: 1-5 business days depending on mobile banking service

5.5 Refund Status Tracking

You can track your refund:

  • Check email confirmation for refund reference number
  • Login to your mobile banking app to see status
  • Contact us for refund status updates
  • Provide order and refund reference numbers

6. Subscription Cancellation

6.1 When Subscriptions Can Be Cancelled

Subscription products have specific cancellation rules:

  • Monthly Subscriptions: Cancel anytime; no charge for future months
  • Annual Subscriptions: Cancel within 30 days for refund; after 30 days, no refund but access continues

6.2 Canceling Recurring Subscriptions

To cancel auto-renewal:

  2. Provide subscription order number
  3. Request cancellation of auto-renewal
  4. Receive confirmation that future charges are blocked

  • Access may be immediately revoked upon refund
